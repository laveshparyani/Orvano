About ORVANO

Crafting Traditional Elegance Since Our Inception

Our Heritage

ORVANO was born from a deep appreciation for traditional Indian craftsmanship and the timeless elegance of old-money aesthetics. Founded by visionaries who understood that true luxury lies in the intersection of heritage and contemporary sophistication, we've dedicated ourselves to preserving the art of traditional Indian tailoring while adapting it for the modern gentleman.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief: that every man deserves to experience the refined elegance that comes from garments crafted with attention to detail, premium materials, and time-honored techniques passed down through generations.

Our Core Values

Craftsmanship

Every garment is a testament to our commitment to exceptional quality and attention to detail.

Heritage

We honor and preserve traditional Indian tailoring techniques while embracing modern innovation.

Luxury

We believe luxury should be accessible to those who appreciate quality, not just those who can afford it.
